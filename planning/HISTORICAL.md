# Historical archive — rldyourterm v1.0 planning corpus (2026-02)

Everything under `planning/` and `metrics/` documents the February 2026
design-lock of the from-scratch `rldyourterm` engine (VSA Rust workspace:
portable-pty + winit + wgpu; stability > AI-CLI compatibility > speed;
no multiplexer, no tabs by contract). The engine reached an MVP scaffold
(pinned here as the `rldyourterm/` submodule at `550d78f`, 2026-03-19) and
was then parked.

These documents are kept as engineering history and as ADR raw material for
any future revival. They are NOT the current contract of this repository —
the current contract is zsh-first tooling curation; see the root `AGENTS.md`
and `TOOLKIT.md`. Nothing in here should be "fixed" to match the present.
